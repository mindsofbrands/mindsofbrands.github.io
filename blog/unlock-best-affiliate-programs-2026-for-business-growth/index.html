What are Affiliate Programs and How Do They Work?

Affiliate programs are arrangements where an online merchant or advertiser pays a commission to an affiliate for promoting their products or services. The affiliate earns a commission for each sale, lead, or referral made through their unique affiliate link. The Best Affiliate Programs 2026 offer a range of benefits, including access to a large network of affiliates, tracking and reporting tools, and competitive commission rates. For example, Amazon's affiliate program is one of the most popular and successful affiliate programs in the world, with millions of products to promote and a reliable tracking system. When choosing an affiliate program, consider the commission rates, cookie duration, and the types of products or services being promoted. Look for programs that offer competitive rates, longer cookie durations, and a diverse range of products that align with your niche or target audience.
