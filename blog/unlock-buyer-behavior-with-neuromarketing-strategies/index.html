What is Neuromarketing?

Neuromarketing is a field of study that combines neuroscience, psychology, and marketing to understand how people make decisions. It uses techniques such as functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and electroencephalography (EEG) to measure brain activity and understand what drives consumer behavior. By applying Neuromarketing principles, businesses can create marketing campaigns that are more effective and efficient. So, how can you apply Neuromarketing principles to your business? Start by understanding your target audience's motivations and emotions. Another example is using the power of anchoring, where you use a higher-priced product to make a lower-priced product seem more affordable. This tactic can increase sales by up to 15%.
