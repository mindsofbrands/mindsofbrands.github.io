What are Decentralized Apps (DApps)?

Decentralized Apps (DApps) are applications that run on a blockchain network, allowing for secure, transparent, and decentralized data management. Unlike traditional apps, DApps operate on a peer-to-peer network, eliminating the need for intermediaries and enabling users to interact directly with the application. This decentralized architecture provides a range of benefits, including enhanced security, increased transparency, and improved scalability. For instance, a decentralized social media platform can ensure that user data is protected and not controlled by a single entity.
