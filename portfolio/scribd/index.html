Scribd Audio Ebooks — SEO Growth Case Study

Ranking Fiction and Business Audiobooks on Google’s Top Search Results

Project Overview

Scribd wanted to improve the visibility and search ranking of its Fiction and Business audiobooks within just 2 months. Our goal was to make Scribd’s audiobook listings dominate the top results for high-intent keywords on Google Search.

Our Strategy

  • Performed detailed keyword research for Fiction and Business audiobook niches.
  • Optimized audiobook titles, meta descriptions, and structured data for Google indexing.
  • Created engaging content snippets for better SERP click-through rates.
  • Built high-quality backlinks through book review blogs and audio content networks.
  • Integrated schema for “Audiobook” and “Author” to boost visibility in search features.

Execution Timeline

  • Week 1: SEO audit, keyword gap analysis, and on-page optimization setup.
  • Week 2–4: Content updates, backlink acquisition, and technical SEO fixes.
  • Week 5–8: Monitoring keyword movements and continuous optimization.

Results Achieved

  • +120% increase in organic visibility in 2 months.
  • Top 3 Google rankings for 10+ high-volume ebook keywords.
  • 60% higher click-through rate on targeted audiobook listings.
  • 35% growth in paid and organic subscription conversions.

Key Takeaway

Within a short timeframe, Scribd’s audiobook catalog became more discoverable and engaging for users searching for specific genres like Fiction and Business audiobooks — solidifying Scribd’s dominance in the audio reading market.
