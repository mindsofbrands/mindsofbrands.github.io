Problem Statement

Scribd needed to rank selected Fiction and Business audiobooks on Google Search within 2 months to capture high-intent readers and increase subscriptions. Key challenges included highly competitive keywords and inadequate schema markup for audio content.

Strategy & Execution

We executed a focused sprint targeting on-page SEO, structured data, content snippets and authoritative link placements to accelerate ranking signals.

  • Keyword + intent mapping: prioritized high-conversion short- and long-tail keywords for fiction & business audiobooks.
  • On-page optimization: updated titles, meta descriptions, H-tags and internal linking for prioritized audiobook pages.
  • Schema & rich snippets: implemented Audiobook/CreativeWork schema, author schema and audioObject markup to improve SERP presentation.
  • Content snippets: crafted concise, search-optimized descriptions and Q&A snippets to target featured snippets and People Also Ask (PAA).
  • Link building: acquired editorial links from book review sites, podcast editors and audio content aggregators.

2-Month Roadmap (Glance)

  • Week 1 — Audit & Keyword Mapping

  • Weeks 2–3 — On-page & Schema

  • Weeks 4–6 — Content & Outreach
    Published targeted content snippets and secured editorial backlinks from niche book sites.

    Tracked ranking changes, adjusted content and expanded link acquisition for momentum.

Fast, surgical SEO implementation — focusing on schema, intent-led content and quality links — can move competitive content into top SERP positions quickly when coordinated effectively.
