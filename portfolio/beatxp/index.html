Category: Wearable Tech | Duration: 5 Months | Goal: Increase smartwatch sales and brand reach

Overview

BeatXP, a wearable technology brand, approached Minds Of Brands to increase its smartwatch sales over a 5-month period. The challenge was to boost conversions while keeping customer acquisition costs minimal across digital platforms.

Strategy

  • Planned a 5-month digital strategy combining Google Ads, Meta Ads, and influencer collaborations.
  • Segmented audiences based on lifestyle and interest metrics to optimize targeting.
  • Launched smartwatch comparison campaigns highlighting fitness accuracy and long battery life.
  • Utilized remarketing ads to re-engage visitors and abandoned cart users.
  • Created seasonal content aligned with fitness trends to boost brand relevance.

Results & Key Metrics

  • +65% Growth in Smartwatch Sales
  • 40% Lower Cost per Acquisition (CPA)
  • 3.2x Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
  • +120% Website Traffic Growth
  • 5M+ Impressions across social campaigns

Conclusion

By executing a structured ad strategy and leveraging consumer behavior insights, BeatXP achieved exceptional growth in smartwatch sales while expanding its brand presence digitally.
