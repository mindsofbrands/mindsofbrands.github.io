A focused performance marketing program combining paid ads, CRO, ASO research and influencer content to scale sales for selected BeatXP smartwatch models in a crowded market.

Work with us See more case studies

Problem Statement

The client wanted to increase sales for select BeatXP smartwatch SKUs by 40% within 5 months and build sustainable repeat purchase behavior. The main challenge: a saturated market with strong competitors and a need to improve conversion efficiency across channels.

Strategy & Execution

We designed a multi-channel growth plan focused on the purchase funnel — awareness, consideration and conversion — and executed iterative experiments across creative, landing pages and audience segments.

  • Market & competitor research: keyword gap analysis across marketplaces and D2C funnels; price-positioning insights.
  • Performance marketing: Google Shopping + Search + YouTube prospecting; Meta conversion campaigns; rigorous A/B creative testing.
  • CRO: optimized product pages, faster checkout flow and structured data for product snippets.
  • Influencer program: 10+ micro-influencers (tech & fitness) for short-form reviews driving tracked traffic to product pages.
  • Automation: email and WhatsApp flows for abandoned carts, post-purchase retention and flash offers.

These results were achieved through continuous creative optimisation, tight bid & audience controls, landing page improvements and a measurable influencer program. The campaigns were scaled while maintaining acquisition efficiency.

5-Month Roadmap (Glance)

  • Month 0 — Research & Setup
    Competitor audit, keyword mapping, analytics & tracking setup, baseline CRO tests.
  • Month 1 — Launch Phase
    Prospecting campaigns across Google & Meta, influencer seeding, initial landing page variants.
  • Month 2 — Optimize
    Creative swaps, audience narrowing, push for higher-converting placements.
  • Month 3 — Scale
    Increase budget on winning ad sets, wider influencer focus, retargeting sequences.
  • Month 4 — Retain
    Post-purchase flows, loyalty messaging, repeat purchase offers & measurement.

Key Takeaway

Precision targeting, iterative creative testing and a mix of paid + influencer content allowed BeatXP to drive strong growth in a competitive category. Short, data-led experiments delivered scalable wins without sacrificing efficiency.
