Betterhalf.ai — 6-Month Play Store Growth

Client: Betterhalf.ai
•
Duration: 6 months
•
Focus: ASO • App installs • Ratings growth

A complete App Store Optimization (ASO) project to enhance organic visibility, keyword rankings, installs, and retention for the Betterhalf.ai app on the Google Play Store.

Work with us See more case studies

Problem Statement

Betterhalf.ai wanted to improve its Play Store visibility and ranking among top dating apps while reducing CPI and boosting organic installs. The key challenge: increasing discoverability in a highly competitive app category while maintaining strong user ratings.

Strategy & Execution

  • Keyword optimization: Researched high-volume search terms and restructured title, short description, and tags.
  • Creative revamp: Optimized app screenshots, icon, and promo videos to improve conversion rates.
  • Review management: Implemented prompt timing and sentiment analysis to boost 4+ star reviews.
  • Localization: Added multilingual metadata (Hindi, Tamil, Bengali) for regional discovery.
  • Ad synergy: Combined paid UA campaigns with ASO experiments for compounding effect.
